French Press

The French Press is a coffee workhorse. It's simple, sustainable, pretty easy to clean, and makes coffee with a lot of body. This has become a go-to method for my wife and I because it can brew coffee for both of us in one go.
Kevin Weeks

Moka Pot Affogato

Affogatos are one of the most delicious things in the entire universe. Hot espresso poured over a generous scoop of ice cream. You get the sensation of hot and cold, and the flavor fuses into sweet, creamy, caramel-y goodness. Best of all, you can make it at home.
Kevin Weeks
