Trader Joe's Kenya AA

Quick Reference

  • Price: $6.99 for 13oz ($0.54/oz)
  • Roast: Medium
  • Flavors: Chocolate
  • Recommended Brewer: French Press

For the price, you can't do much better than Trader Joe's Kenya AA - $7 for 13 ounces! The chocolatey flavor stands strong, so if you like chocolate, you will love this bean.

It's got a deeper flavor so it's a great bean for a full-bodied brewing method like the French Press. I go for 3:30 ground as course as I can with a 1:12 ratio and it is delicious.

Kevin Weeks
